Giving Back to the Community

Gobble Doggs Scholarship

2025 Scholarship Now Open!

Applications must be postmarked no later than Friday, June 12, 2026.

Gobble Doggs is happy to announce its 2025 Gobble Doggs Scholarship application is open. A Gobble Doggs scholarship will be offered to at least one scholarship recipient that will assist a student in their educational and/or entrepreneurial goals.

Important Note:

You do not have to attend a college or university to apply for the Gobble Doggs Scholarship. The scholarship can be used for educational support, advancing an existing business, or developing a business concept. In other words, if you cut or braid hair on the side, cut lawns, type papers, walk dogs, etc., you can apply for the scholarship to assist in your entrepreneurial goals.

The Gobble Doggs scholarship will be awarded to an African American graduating high school senior who is innovative, forward-thinking, has a passion for business and entrepreneurship. Today, we see more students seeking opportunities to minimize their college debt. Some may opt not to attend college at all but pursue a trade and/or start their own business.

We certainly want to encourage entrepreneurship in any capacity by giving a student funds to assist in pursuing their lifelong dream.

Gobble Doggs is based in the Chicagoland area and is a family-owned business, serving delicious gourmet turkey and vegan hot dogs. We have grown since we opened in 2012 and have long believed that our success is not just based on sales but also our community involvement, which is why we have been giving away this scholarship since 2019.

Winners will be announced in June. We encourage all qualifying students to apply! We wish every applicant much luck and success in pursuit of their entrepreneurial dreams. Remember to dream big and often!
